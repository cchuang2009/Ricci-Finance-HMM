# Future Development

Planned research directions include:

- probabilistic forecasting of graph-level features;
- Toto 2 or GluonTS benchmark adapters;
- directed lead–lag and capital-flow graphs;
- persistent cluster identity across frames;
- walk-forward validation and calibration;
- regime-transition probability visualization;
- exportable animation and lecture reports;
- unit and regression tests for every module.

Ricci flow should remain labeled as geometric regularization unless future graph
inputs are produced by an explicit forecasting model.
